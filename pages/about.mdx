# About

Wackerspace is brought to you by [Mikey](https://mikey.nz) of [Art~Hack](https://arthack.nz/), a social gathering for creative tech in Wellington, which has been active since 2014.

Wellington is missing a long-term 24/7-access community space with substantial tools to make things, we want to help make this happen!
